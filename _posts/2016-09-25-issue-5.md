Heidelberg Laureate Forum
=========================
Last week, giants of computer science gave lectures at the Heidelberg Laureate Forum. 
The [videos are online](https://www.youtube.com/user/LaureateForum) and including [this lecture on abstractions and CS history by Barbara Liskov](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fX7hrszOAN0)
which calls on programmers to call on the history of programming and read early papers.

Johana Parker [compiled a list of the papers referenced](http://jpirker.com/hlf16-liskovs-reading-list-for-computer-scientists/) by Liskov, with links.

Functional at Scale
===================
This week in ACMQueue: [Functional at Scale](https://queue.acm.org/detail.cfm?id=3001119), Marius Eriksen dives into how Twitter uses Functional Programming
to build scalable, distributed systems. Discussion of futures, higher order Functions, coordination, and examples in Scala.
